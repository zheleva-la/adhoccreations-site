About Ad Hoc Creations

Bespoke CNC woodworking — built around your idea, finished with care.

Ad Hoc Creations creates custom wooden pieces — from wedding signage and house signs to practical builds and one-off commissions. We combine CNC precision with thoughtful hand finishing to keep the warmth and character of natural timber.

“Ad Hoc” means the work is driven by the brief — each build is made to order, designed around your space, your event, or your needs.
